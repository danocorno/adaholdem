ADA Hold 'Em

♤ About

Texas Hold 'Em tournaments operate weekly at Austin bars. The game is free to play, with cash prizes for nightly tournament winners.

♡ Location and Time

♧ Sundays

  • Highland Lounge
  • 4:30 P.M. Sign Up, 5:00 P.M. Start

♢ Mondays

  • Highland Lounge
  • 7:30 P.M. Sign Up, 8:00 P.M. Start
  • Cash-jackpot available to the first player each game to get 4-of-a-kind with pocket pairs, straight flush, or royal flush.

♤ Fridays

  • Oilcan Harry's
  • 6:30 P.M. Sign Up, 7:00 P.M. Start play
  • Cash-jackpot available to the first player each game to get 4-of-a-kind with pocket pairs, straight flush, or royal flush.

♤ Announcements, and Event Enrollments

♧ Player Standings

♧ Get More Chips

Play starts with 3000 in chips. Extra chips can be obtained through various incentives.

  • 700 bonus chips for each beverage purchased from the bar
  • 500 bonus chips for reserving a spot at the table via Facebook or Google+ event page
  • 500 bonus chips for bringing a friend who is new to our games, plus the friend gets 500 too
  • 1000 bonus chips once during the week of your birthday
