ADA House Rules

Welcome to ADA HOLD'EM 

House Rules

  • Starting chips is 3000
    Option for bonus chips:  
  1. Facebook-Event Join:  500
  2. Be On time at game start:  1000
  3. Birthday week, one time:  1000
  4. Bring a friend:  500 each, you & friend
  • Re-entry:  3 person min. consent
  • Players are expected to be respectful of all players and dealers.
  • Dealer will not look at players’ cards unless asked by player.
  • Jackpot requirements: Pocket pair in hand to win with quads. One of 5 in hand for straight flush. Hand must play out until showdown.
  • Players that show their cards to anyone will be asked to fold their cards.
  • Coaching is not allowed.
  • Player must be at the table, or their cards will be folded.
  • Chips placed on playing area will be considered a bet.
  • Players will only act when it is their turn.
  • Players that push cards in, forfeit their hand.
